THE HURDLE

Peak Season Reliability.

For a seasonal brand like Evershine, 80% of annual traffic occurs within a 3-month monsoon window. The primary challenge was building a platform that remains unshakeable during high-concurrency peaks.

The client needed to move beyond a basic catalog to a conversion-optimized D2C powerhouse. They required advanced product filtering (by material, size, and gender) and a mobile-first checkout flow that reduced the abandoned cart rate during high-intent shopping periods.

IconTech architected a custom technical roadmap—unifying technical SEO for competitive rankings with a scalable Shopify-Plus infrastructure to ensure Evershine dominates the Indian rainwear market year-round.
