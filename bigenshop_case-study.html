THE HURDLE

Trust vs. Technology.

Bigen’s reputation is built on ammonia-free, natural-look hair coloring. The challenge was translating this tactile, trust-based retail experience into a digital interface for the sophisticated French consumer.

The project required a technical architecture that could manage a complex catalog—ranging from permanent powder colors to semi-permanent creams—while maintaining sub-second load times to satisfy Google’s Core Web Vitals and European UX expectations.

IconTech architected a custom technical foundation—unifying color-accurate visual storytelling with a robust logistics backend to ensure the transition from physical shelf to digital cart was seamless and high-converting.
