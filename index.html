Absolutely. IconTech Studio provides 100% ownership of the source code and intellectual property rights to you upon project completion. We build your future, not our portfolio.

Yes. We are long-term partners. After your MVP impacts the market, we provide dedicated support for feature iterations, performance optimization, and global scaling strategies.

We provide fixed-price roadmap estimates after our initial scoping phase. This ensures transparency and helps you budget effectively without worrying about hidden hourly fees.
