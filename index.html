+

+

Absolutely. IconTech Studio provides 100% ownership of the source code and intellectual property rights to you upon project completion. We build your future, not our portfolio.

+

We use industry-leading modern stacks to ensure scalability. This includes React/Next.js for web, Flutter or React Native for mobile apps, and robust Node.js or Python backends with cloud-native infrastructure.

Can you help with scaling after the launch?

+

Yes. We are long-term partners. After your product impacts the market, we provide dedicated support for feature iterations, performance optimization, and global scaling strategies.

How much will my project cost?

+

We provide fixed-price roadmap estimates after our initial scoping phase. This ensures transparency and helps you budget effectively without worrying about hidden hourly fees.
